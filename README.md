## 1. Foundation: Creating Your AI Girl

### 1.1 Choose Your Generator
Use **[THIS tool](https://aliveai.app/?affiliate_id=MlzrL)** to generate realistic pictures and videos of your girl.

> **Important:** It can feel overwhelming at first. Dedicate at least 3-5 days purely to learning the tool before going live. Patience here pays off massively.

### 1.2 Craft a Consistent Character Sheet
Before generating anything, define every detail so she looks like the *same person* across all images. Create a document with:

| Category | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Face** | Eye color, nose shape, lip fullness, freckles, beauty marks, jawline |
| **Body** | Height, build, skin tone, tattoos, scars, unique features |
| **Hair** | Color, length, texture, usual style (down, ponytail, etc.) |
| **Vibe** | Girl-next-door, goth, fitness model, shy bookworm, etc. |
| **Wardrobe** | 5-7 go-to outfits she rotates through (consistency = realism) |
| **Environment** | Her bedroom, favorite café, gym, beach — recurring locations |

### 1.3 Build a "Media Bank" Before Launch
Stockpile at least **50-100 images** and **10-15 short videos** before you ever post a link.

- **Why:** When curious users click through, they need to see a full, lived-in profile, not 3 empty posts.
- **Content mix to prepare:**
  - Casual selfies (mirror, car, couch)
  - Lifestyle shots (coffee, reading, walking dog)
  - "Candid" moments (laughing, messy hair, waking up)
  - 2-3 slightly suggestive teasers (never full explicit on free socials)
  - Short looping video clips (5-10 seconds) — *video builds 10x more trust than photos*

## 3. Content Production Pipeline

### 3.1 The 80/20 Content Rule
- **80% lifestyle & personality** — Makes her feel real. Men don't just pay for content; they pay for the *fantasy of connection*.
- **20% suggestive/tease** — Keeps them curious and upgrading to paid tiers.

### 3.2 Batch Creation Schedule
- **Monday:** Generate 1 week's worth of images (20-30)
- **Tuesday:** Generate 3-5 short videos/clips
- **Wednesday:** Write captions for the week using her "voice"
- **Weekly total output:** ~4-7 posts per platform per day

### 3.3 Story-Driven Content Ideas
People follow *narratives*, not just pretty pictures. Create story arcs:

- "Morning routine" series
- "Getting ready for a date" (but she's single — mystery!)
- "Weekend getaway" photo dump
- Polls: "Which outfit should I wear tonight?"
- "Cooking disaster" blooper-style video
- AMA (Ask Me Anything) story sessions

## 4. Social Media Domination Strategy

### 4.1 Platform-Specific Tactics

#### Instagram
- **Post frequency:** 2-3x daily (feed), 5-10 stories/day
- **Reels are king:** 3-5 Reels per week minimum — trending audio + casual/beauty shots
- **Growth hacks:**
  - Collab posts with other AI models (cross-pollinate audiences)
  - Use 5-10 niche hashtags (e.g., #blondebeauty, #fitgirls, not oversaturated ones)
  - Reply to EVERY comment for the first hour after posting (algorithm boost)
  - Stories with polls, sliders, and questions to farm engagement

#### X (Twitter)
- **Post frequency:** 5-10x daily — the timeline moves fast
- **Content style:** Witty, flirty text posts + 1 image/gif per post
- **Growth hacks:**
  - Jump into trending conversations with a flirty take
  - Quote-tweet viral posts with her "reaction"
  - Post "thirst trap" text tweets that get quote-retweeted
  - Use communities (NSFW-friendly ones) to reach target audiences

#### TikTok
- **Post frequency:** 2-3x daily
- **Content style:** Trending sounds, POV videos, "outfit checks," transition edits
- **Growth hacks:**
  - First 3 seconds must hook HARD (movement, text hook, question)
  - Use CapCut for smooth AI-girl transitions
  - Comment on large creators' posts early with clever replies
  - Never mention Fanvue or external links — funnel to IG first, then bio link

#### Reddit
- **Warning:** Reddit users are more tech-savvy and skeptical. Tread carefully.
- **Target subreddits:** r/selfie, r/Faces, r/amihot, niche fetish subs, r/Instagrammodels
- **Rules:**
  - NEVER spam Fanvue links in titles
  - Post genuine-looking casual selfies with relatable titles
  - Link in your Reddit profile bio, not the post
  - Engage in comments like a real person
  - Delete and repost if a post flops (different time, different title)

### 4.2 The Link-in-Bio Funnel
Set up a **Linktree** or **Beacons** page containing:

1. Fanvue link (top, highlighted)
2. Instagram backup account
3. Telegram channel
4. Amazon wishlist
5. "About me" section with her story

**Crucial:** Rotate the link in bio weekly — sometimes go direct to Fanvue, sometimes to a "free trial" Telegram, sometimes to a specific PPV post. This prevents fatigue and tests conversion paths.

1. User comments "hey" or fire emoji on IG post
2. You (or bot) DM them a *mildly flirty* but tame message
3. After 3-4 messages of banter, softly mention "I chat more on my other page"
4. Link to Fanvue

### 5.2 Cross-Pollination with Other AI Models

- Create "roommate" content with another model (both accounts post the same "candid" scene from different angles)

## 8. Risk Management & Legal Cover

### 8.1 Platform Survival Guide
- **Instagram shadowban avoidance:** Never use banned hashtags. Rotate hashtag sets. Avoid explicit language in captions.
- **TikTok:** Anything suggestive goes to "yellow" status fast. Keep it PG-13.
- **Reddit:** Read each subreddit's rules carefully. Many will permaban on first offense.
- **Fanvue:** They handle AI disclosure for you. Do NOT break their TOS by pretending to be a real human if directly asked by support.

### 8.2 Tax & Business Setup
- Open a separate bank account for all income
- Track EVERY expense (AI tool subscriptions, chatters, ads)
- Register as a sole proprietor or LLC (depending on your country)
- Set aside **25-30%** of all revenue for taxes
- Hire an accountant who understands digital/OnlyFans-style businesses
- **Yes, this is all legal if you pay your taxes.**

### 8.3 Backup & Redundancy
- Maintain a backup Instagram account (always growing slowly)
- Build an email list via a "free photo" signup landing page
- Have a Telegram channel as your "home base" — platforms can ban you overnight, but Telegram is more resilient
- Store all generated content in cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox) organized by date and outfit
